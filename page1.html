Here at PAWS, we urge you to use pet adoption instead of shopping at a pet store. Everyday, over 4,100 dogs are killed in America’s shelters simply because they don’t have safe places to call home. Many dogs from across the United States experience inhumane situations: overcrowding, abusive owners, or hoarding. Dogs from pet stores typically come from puppy mills. Puppy mills are establishments where dogs are bred uncarefully and in poor conditions (where they do not receive the proper care that they need). And oftentimes, these puppy mill dogs come with neurological problems or common illnesses. By adopting By adopting, it means that each time you look into your pet’s eyes, you can feel good knowing that you truly did save a life.
