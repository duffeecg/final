Who are we?

We are a dog adoption group that guarantees to find you your PAW-fect friend. Each year, we help 10,000 dogs get the care and attention that they so desperately need. PAWS is working to transform the future of dog shelters by providing you with personalized guidance, which will help you through every step of the adoption process. As a pet-loving community, we can work together to advocate for animal rights. We can create a more humane environment and we can support dogs at every stage of life.

Here at PAWS, we believe that every dog deserves a happy home. We make it our missuon to match the right dog with the right family. We praise ourselves on being good to animals and partnering with people. By focusing on both the animals and the humans, we learn to lead responsibly and compassionately. Our Twin Cities adoption centers are the perfect location. By being centered in such a large metropolitan area, more dogs and more lives can be changed.
